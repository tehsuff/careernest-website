INSPIRING SOCIAL INCLUSION THROUGH TECHNOLOGY

For more than 43 years, WorkVentures has been committed to empowering organisations and individuals through technology.

about WorkVentures

WorkVentures is Australia's leading IT social enterprise. We are committed to creating inclusive, vibrant communities.

We help young people gain meaningful skills, training, and jobs and provide affordable technology and connectivity to those who are digitally excluded. We also combat e-waste and promote a circular economy through our device refurbishment and deliver social procurement services to our customers.

Our Solutions

Youth Employment Solutions

WorkVentures' Youth Employment Solutions provides sustainable employment opportunities for all young Australians.

We offer a range of options, including traineeships, career pathway programs, work placement and cyber cadetships.

IT Solutions

WorkVentures' IT Solutions team focuses on closing the digital divide by providing low-cost, high-quality technology to organisations and individuals around Australia.

We offer affordable laptops and PCs as well as managed services for NFP organisations.

Logistics Solutions

WorkVentures' Logistic Solutions team provides a variety of services to meet corporate needs as well as the needs of small businesses.

Our mission is to promote a circular economy through our e-waste recycling program.

Technical Solutions

WorkVentures' Technical Solutions experts have 30 years of experience in providing proficient technical services, high-quality refurbishment of devices, and staging and imaging.

We complete in-house triage, testing, and repairs while securely erasing data.
