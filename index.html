About CareerNest

We're here to help you learn and grow through real-world insights.

In today's fast-changing world, learning only from textbooks is not enough. Real understanding comes from using what you learn in real-life situations. That's why we share practical knowledge based on real experiences, challenges, and successes. Whether you are just beginning or improving your skills, we give you the support and tools to turn knowledge into action.

Our way of teaching is different. We share practical examples related to your field, helping you build strong thinking, flexibility, and problem-solving skills. All profits from our social enterprise are reinvested into digital inclusion and youth employment programs.

About CareerNest

Australia's leading student placement & IT social enterprise, creating inclusive communities.

Empowering Individuals, Creating Opportunities

CareerNest was founded to bridge the gap between education and employment. We provide real-world placement experiences, affordable technology, and youth employment pathways. Our core mission: helping students and job seekers turn knowledge into action while promoting digital inclusion.

All profits are reinvested into programs that help young people gain meaningful skills and jobs.

Youth Employment Solutions

Meaningful Employment & Traineeships for All Young Australians

Career Pathways That Work

We help students and young job seekers find paid traineeships, work placements, and cyber security cadetships. Our program includes one-on-one interview preparation, resume coaching, and ongoing support. With an 88% completion rate (40% above national average), we ensure you're job-ready.
